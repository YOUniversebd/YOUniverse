YOUbiquity

YOUbiquity is the social & environmental responsibility wing of YOUniverse. We work across Bangladesh to promote awareness, empowerment, sustainability and active citizenship through multimedia activism, physical campaigns, and community-based development programs.

About Us

YOUbiquity drives social and environmental change with a unique blend of **visual storytelling**, **community engagement**, and **grassroots activism**. Our philosophy is simple: *real change begins when ordinary people become aware, empowered, and connected to purpose*.

We operate through field activities, educational campaigns, art-based activism, documentary-driven awareness, and youth engagement programs. Our aim is to build a Bangladesh where informed, empowered communities take the lead in positive transformation.
