About YOUbiquity

YOUbiquity is the social impact wing of YOUniverse, designed to merge creative expression with activism. Our mission is to create a culture where storytelling, theatre, visual arts, community leadership, and environmental responsibility come together to bring sustainable and meaningful change in communities across Bangladesh.
