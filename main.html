About YOUniverse

YOUniverse is a creative agency established in Bangladesh in 2017. YOUniverse help and provide you trusted service for local as well as international partner. Our main strength is based on providing our customers with effective visual solutions with a valuable artistic depth. From the concept through production to delivery, we offer the optimum balance of value and experience. Our team of talented photographers, cinematographers, designers and writers serve all areas in Bangladesh. Our approach is one of collaboration. We like to hear your views and reactions to our work. How can we help you?

Photograpy, Videography, Writing services, TV Production, Media Marketing, Design & Printing — one-stop creative solutions

Photography

Our in-house creative photography team made up of professional photographers can add value to your brand, product or event with development photography, event photography, corporate photography, industrial photography and so on.

Videography

With in-house production capabilities, we offer creative video production services. Our video production includes, but is not limited to: Documentary, TVC, OVC, AV, Feature Film, Animation Film, TV Drama, Motion Graphics, Video Editing etc.

Writing services

We are the one stop writing agency for you. Writing services offered include: business, technical, academic, blog, article, travel writing services plus social media management and web content writing among others.

TV Production

We produce any kind of TV Production like TV Drama, Telefilm, TVC, Talk Show, Reality Show, Social Awareness Program and so on. Our dedicated creative team of professionals are ready to play creatively.

Media Marketing

YOUniverse's experienced and trained media buyers know how to work with vendors to get the best media buys for our clients; this includes negotiating the best, added value/bonuses, production incentives, sponsorship opportunities, and more.

Design & printing

Effective and creative graphic design will help you increase the return on your investment (creative and financial), whether that’s an investment in printed tools, online, exhibition or other printed items like business card, leaflet, poster, banner etc. We started our service in printing and design and still deliver results for companies large and small who recognize the value of creative design and layout.
